The internet, an Ethical nightmare

Student Name: Ettienne Swart

Student Number: 1595043

What does this mean to me?

Regarding the internet as an ethical space, it is an environment where responsible and ethical use of technology is important as it has both the power to create and perform amazing things but can absolutely demolish any user who is targeted by malicious users. It is a constantly shifting environment where a user must have respect, integrity, and responsibility to avoid the worst things the internet has to offer.

Personally, I have only experienced a small (thankfully) number of malicious/ethical issues from the internet and if you aren’t well informed in the good online habits to have when using the internet, you can easily fall victim. It reminds me of a recent movie that came out called Beekeeper and the premise is how internet scammers manage to steal an old lady’s management fund (or charity fund I can’t recall) that was around 2 million dollars by tricking her using the bank scammer method to gain access. From this distraught she took her life and her friend, a “Beekeeper” (retired), the only person that the old lady offered help and gave a care about, now was gone and in this wake, the Beekeeper wants vengeance though bloody justice. The Beekeeper comes out from retirement and goes on a brutal campaign for vengeance where he takes on national stakes after he is revealed to be a former operative of a powerful and clandestine organization known as "Beekeepers." Getting back on track, this is a real thing, not the beekeeper, the internet scams and malicious scum of the earth do this every day, every hour.

What does this look like?

From what I have gathered, there are quite a few. The following are a handful of ethical issues that are common:

  • Digital divide and access rights
  • Sales tax on Internet transactions
  • Internet free speech and censorship
  • Regulation of websites with global presence
  • Role of the internet in facilitating globalization
  • Discrimination and harassment online
