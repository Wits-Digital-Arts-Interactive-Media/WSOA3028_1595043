Blog Post 5

In week 6 of our course, (18/03 - 22/03), it was quite interesting to learn about UX and UI. Things like pop up adds that are designed on purpose to be hard to close and instead easy to click the link was mentioned by Hanli. Just one of the many possibilities that websites today can do unethically. It is not surprising to see that in todays world but hoepfully in the near future its growth is stunted. Nevertheless, for my website choice i want to invesitgate my Alma Mater to see if its level of quality in education is also reflected in it website.

The following list below will discuss what i could find from the website, note that it will not go into full detail

Alma Mater Pros

  • Fluent detail, helping users understand fully of what Alma mater has to offer
  • Clever use of aesthetic colour such as shades of blue and red to represent their brand and utilize colour psychology that is trust, calm and bussines

Alma Mater Cons

  • Missing content from certain pages such as the sports webpages not showing anything related to the topic
  • Font size varies yet ultimately due to the amount of content their is text wise, it is a bit small and makes it hard for user readability

All in all the website seems to be meeting many of the requirements and should help me in futhering my own website in regards to aesthetic choices.
