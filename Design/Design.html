Goal Alignment

My goal for the website overall is to achieve a simple, engineering aesthetic that easily communicates all relevant information to my users. Information that doesn't belong in a specific category should not be found and my users should not have a hard time finding what they desire so my structure is similar to that of a 5-ary tree like that of coding. From the above, my main focus of users are Engineers however still pleasing enough for digital artists. So, a simple, calm, and logical target audience is my desire. What I necessarily want my users to see is a unique but simple, not over-the-top where the information they desire is easy to access and interact with. My main focus of the website is my portfolio, Essays, and Blog posts accompanied by my profile, Design, and Experiments that I had performed in my own time. The Blog posts and portfolio showing my progress in life is the most important aspect of the website. The experiments performed along with the Blogs and portfolio are the most interesting.

Style Process:

In regards to the style process, here are the two websites that i will be using for inspiration

  • My Alma Mater
  • HRGreen

Style Guide:

  • Font: Aptos. It is a readable font and although less formal the New Romans, it is suitable for the desire to have it be easily readable
  • Colour Scheme: Using a white background, with black boxes and light green elements (spots of green)
  • Images: There will not be many images, however their size will be biug enough to allow users to easily see the contents of the image
  • Naviagition components will be around heading size to allow users to easily find and use them.
  • The font size will be 13.5 for text and headings will be larger depending on order of hiearchy
  • Background colours will be either a mix of light grey, light blue or light green. These colours have been proven to work well for user readablility and demonstrate professionalism.
